[1.2.0] - 11.07.26
## Changed
- Reworked the upgraded machine system: Upgraded machines (Polarization Tower, Advanced Electric Quarry, etc.) now process recipes of their basic versions at the same EU cost, but slightly faster.
Massive machines now process recipes slightly faster while consuming the same amount of energy as their basic versions, and also now have the ability to process up to 16 recipes simultaneously. (https://github.com/kraneeeee/absolute-industrialization/issues/5)
- Ore sample bounding boxes have been adjusted for convenience.
- Ore samples are now waterlogged.
- Flax and Rice have been unified.
- Added a Drain to the Foundry quest.
